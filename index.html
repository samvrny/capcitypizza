About Us

Welcome to Capitol City Pizza, the heart and soul of downtown Madison, WI! Established by the dynamic trio of Tom "Big Tom" Baker, Sally Fortuna, and Sean Westfall, Capitol City Pizza is more than just a pizza place — it's a local institution rooted in passion, flavor, and community spirit. Our story begins with Big Tom's love for crafting mouthwatering pizzas that capture the essence of Madison's diverse culinary scene. Combined with Sally's expertise in creating delectable wings and Sean's mastery in perfecting sub sandwiches, Capitol City Pizza emerged as a one-stop destination for all things delicious. At Capitol City Pizza, we pride ourselves on using only the finest, locally sourced ingredients to create our signature pizzas. From classic pepperoni to inventive specialty pies, each slice is a testament to our commitment to quality and taste. Whether you're a fan of traditional pepperoni or adventurous enough to try our unique combinations, we have a pizza for every palate. But it's not just about pizza here. Our menu features an array of irresistible wings, perfectly seasoned and cooked to crispy perfection. Dive into the bold flavors of our subs, where fresh ingredients meet artisanal bread for a taste sensation that will keep you coming back for more. We understand the importance of convenience, and that's why Capitol City Pizza offers speedy and reliable delivery services. Whether you're enjoying a night in or hosting a gathering with friends, let us bring the flavors of Capitol City Pizza straight to your doorstep. Join us in our cozy pizzeria, where the aroma of freshly baked pies and the warmth of friendly faces create an inviting atmosphere for pizza enthusiasts and food lovers alike. Capitol City Pizza — where every bite tells a story, and every slice is made with love. Welcome to our pizza family!
